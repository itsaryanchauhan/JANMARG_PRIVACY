Privacy Policy for JanMarg

Last updated: September 20, 2025

1. Introduction

JanMarg ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our civic reporting mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera and Photo Access

We request camera permission to allow you to:

  • Take photos of civic issues (potholes, broken streetlights, etc.) for reports
  • Upload images from your photo gallery to document community problems

2.2 Location Information

We collect your location data to:

  • Automatically tag the location of reported civic issues
  • Show relevant reports in your area
  • Help authorities locate and address problems efficiently

2.3 Report Data

When you submit reports, we collect:

  • Issue descriptions and categories
  • Photos you choose to include
  • Location coordinates
  • Timestamp of reports

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Facilitating civic issue reporting and tracking
  • Enabling community engagement through upvoting and viewing reports
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Communicating with local authorities about reported issues

4. Data Sharing

We may share anonymized report data with:

  • Local government authorities for issue resolution
  • Community members viewing public reports

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Camera Permission Details

The camera permission (android.permission.CAMERA) is used exclusively for:

  • Taking photos of civic issues within the app
  • No background photo capture
  • No automatic photo uploads without user action
  • Photos are only used for the specific reports you create

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:

Email: privacy@janmarg.app

App: JanMarg Civic Reports

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

This privacy policy ensures compliance with Google Play Store requirements for apps using camera permissions.
